# Vehicle image notes

The active `*-studio.jpg` fleet images were generated specifically for the Jidor Car demo. They share one controlled studio setup, front-left three-quarter camera angle, lighting direction and color grade. They require no external photo attribution.

The older real-world reference photographs below are retained in the repository but are no longer displayed on the homepage.
